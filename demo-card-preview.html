The Current Beneath Still Waters
where depth moves unseen
You carry the ocean in small gestures,
a tide that others feel but cannot name.
What looks like stillness
is actually patience,
the kind that reshapes shores
one grain at a time.
Where does your quiet power move most freely?
echoes may land past, present, or future
